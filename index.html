Carlos Yulo

Filipino artistic gymnast

Carlos Yulo cops Olympic double gold in the Paris Olympics 2024

Timeline (2000-2017)

Early Life and Education

  • Born on February 16, 2000 in Manila Philippines as the second of four siblings
  • Started training for gymnastics at the Gymnastics Association of the Philippines (GAP) at seven years old
  • Attended Adamson University for high school under the help of GAP
  • Accepted an offer by the Japan Olympic Association in 2016 to train under a scholarship program in Teikyo University

Early Career

  • 2009 Palarong Pambansa in Tacloban
    Placed fifth and second in different categories
  • 2010 Palarong Pambansa in Tarlac City
    Gold in individual all-around and floor exercise, Bronze in vault
  • 2012 Palarong Pambansa in Pangasinan
    Gold in individual all-around, floor exercise, and vault
  • 2013 Palarong Pambansa in Negros Oriental
    Gold in team event, individual all-around, and floor exercise, Silver in vault
  • 2014 ASEAN School Games
    Gold in floor exercise and parallel bars
  • 2014 Pacific Rim Championships
    Sixth in floor exercise, Eigth in vault
  • 2015 International Junior Competition in Yokohama, Japan
    Bronze in vault
  • 2016 Pacific Rim Championship
    Gold in floor exercise and vault, Silver in still rings and parallel bars, Bronze in all-around
  • 2017 Junior Asian Championships
    Gold in parallel bars despite ankle injury
  • 2017 International Junior Competition
    Gold in vault final, Silver in floor exercise final
